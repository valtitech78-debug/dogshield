How AI Works
DogShield uses Claude AI (Anthropic) to analyze foods, plants and substances. Responses are based on veterinary toxicology databases and are cross-referenced with curated safety data. AI can make mistakes — always verify with a licensed veterinarian for emergencies.
⚠️ Important Disclaimer
DogShield AI provides informational guidance only and is NOT a substitute for professional veterinary advice. In case of emergency, contact your vet or an emergency animal hospital immediately. The app cannot diagnose conditions or guarantee accuracy of AI-generated responses.
