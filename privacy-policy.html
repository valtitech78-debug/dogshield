Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 30, 2026 · Effective immediately

This Privacy Policy describes how DogShield AI ("we", "our", or "the app"), developed by Massimiliano Merlo, collects, uses, and protects your information when you use the DogShield application.

1. Information We Collect

DogShield is designed with privacy in mind. The app operates primarily on-device and collects minimal data:

  • Dog profile data — name, breed, weight, age, sex, and photo you provide. Stored locally on your device.
  • Check history — foods and substances you search for, stored locally on your device.
  • Health reminders — vaccination and medication reminders you create, stored locally.
  • Weight logs — weight entries you add manually, stored locally.
  • Emergency vet number — stored locally on your device only.

We do not collect your name, email address, location, or any personally identifiable information unless you contact us directly.

2. AI-Powered Analysis

When you scan a food, plant, or substance, the image or text is sent to Anthropic's Claude AI API for analysis. This data is processed according to Anthropic's Privacy Policy. We do not store or retain the content of your scans on our servers.

AI responses are educational and informational only — they are not veterinary advice.

3. Purchases and Subscriptions

DogShield uses Apple In-App Purchases for premium subscriptions. Payment processing is handled entirely by Apple. We do not collect or store any payment or billing information. For subscription management, please refer to your Apple ID settings.

4. Data Storage

All personal data (dog profiles, history, reminders) is stored locally on your device. We do not operate a user database or cloud sync service. If you delete the app, all locally stored data is permanently removed.

5. Third-Party Services

DogShield integrates the following third-party services:

  • Anthropic Claude API — AI analysis of foods and substances. Privacy Policy
  • Apple App Store / StoreKit — subscription and purchase management. Privacy Policy
  • RevenueCat — subscription status management. Privacy Policy

6. Children's Privacy

DogShield is not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe a child has provided us with personal information, please contact us and we will delete it promptly.

7. Data Security

Since all data is stored locally on your device, your information is protected by your device's built-in security. We recommend keeping your device updated and protected with a passcode.

8. Your Rights

Since we do not collect personal data on our servers, there is no account to delete. All data you have provided is on your device and can be removed by deleting the app. For any privacy concerns, please contact us.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of significant changes through the app or by updating the "Last updated" date above. Continued use of the app after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
