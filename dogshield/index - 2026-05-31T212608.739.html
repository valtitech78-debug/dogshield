How it works

Three seconds to know
if your dog is safe

1

Snap or type

Point the camera at a suspicious snack or plant — or just type its name. DogShield recognizes hundreds of foods, plants and household substances.

2

AI assesses the risk

It cross-references a vet-reviewed toxicology database and weighs the real risk for your dog's breed, weight and the amount eaten.

3

Act with confidence

Get a clear safe / caution / high-concern verdict, a plain-English explanation, and one tap to call your emergency vet if it matters.
